Cherry Limeade Cocktail

This was shamelessly ripped off a bottle of cherry limeade flavored vodka. All credits to...Svedka?

Ingredients

  • 1 part grenadine
  • 1 part cherry limeade flavored vodka more is probably better
  • 2 parts limeade more is definitely better
  • water, lime juice, salt as necessary to balance it out
  • Ice to equal volume of liquid

Instructions

Shaken

  1. Mix main ingredients
  2. Shake, pour over ice

Blended

  1. Add liquid ingredients to blender
  2. Add ice until it no longer floats
  3. Blend, enjoy
