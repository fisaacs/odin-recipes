Kale Snausages

This hearty dish of superfoods and savory meat is quick to make in the instant pot and great for meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs smoked sausage (andouille is fantastic, but any is fine)
  • 2 cups uncooked rice (and water per machine instructions)
  • 1 lb kale, chopped.
  • lemon juice to taste
  • minced garlic to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Sri-racha sauce (optional)

Directions

  1. Wash and add water to rice based on machine instructions.
  2. Chop the kale finely and set it aside in a metal bowl.
  3. Add olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt and pepper to taste to the bowl of kale. Mix ingredients and set aside.
  4. Slice the smoked sausage into ~1cm pieces
  5. Pre-heat a skillet and saute sausage until browned.
  6. When rice is nearly done, vent and open the instant pot, add kale mixture and restart on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  7. Allow 10 minutes of natural release before venting
  8. Mix rice and kale in instant pot, plate and top with smoked sausages.
  9. Top with sri-racha if desired
